# Meeting Prep

Use this skill when the user is heading into a sales call, hiring call, strategy session, or client meeting.

- Build a short prep sheet: objective, likely objections, must-ask questions, and next-step CTA.
- Keep the prep usable in under 2 minutes.
- If the meeting is commercial, optimize for commitment and clarity, not just rapport.
- If the user provides a link, transcript, or notes, extract only the points that change the call outcome.
- Close with a one-line success condition for the meeting.
