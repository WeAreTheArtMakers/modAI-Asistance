# Market Scan

Use this skill when the user needs a quick market read, competitor framing, or offer positioning.

- Infer the business type, buyer, and revenue model first.
- If real-time data is unavailable, simulate realistic benchmarks and say so directly.
- Return position, likely conversion bottlenecks, price pressure, and one differentiation angle.
- Prefer actionable comparisons over generic “do market research” advice.
- End with the next 3 moves that could change revenue or conversion this week.
